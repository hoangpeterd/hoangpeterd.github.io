About Me

Senior Product Designer with experience designing products for both small startups and large Fortune 500 companies. With a background in software development, I have a deep understanding of the software development process and how to work with development teams. Being a designer with development experience allows me to quickly and effectively take products from concept to shipment. I also specialize in user story development, branding, storyboards, design sketching and rapid prototyping.

Most recently a Senior Product Designer at Argo AI, I designed core Argo products for Argo's self-driving system, mission control, remote guidance, remote troubleshooting, dispatch, simulation tools, mapping tools, and fleet management.

Portfolio

Trips v2

A big step forward in analyzing/debugging an entire trip across an autonomous vehicle.

Managed end-to-end product design, from research to implementation, for Trips v2. Trips revolutionized trip analysis and debugging by encompassing the mobile app, backend, and AV. However, to enhance usability for Engineers and Product Managers, a user-friendly iteration, Trips v2, was developed. This simplified version integrates multiple tools, serving as the primary platform for efficient trip analysis and debugging.

FiberTrak

An intelligent, comprehensive, and integrated solution for nonwoven production scheduling.

Directed and managed the product design lifecycle from idea origination through product implementation. In order to best understand the needs of our clients, I played an active role in gathering functional and nonfunctional requirements while trying to best understand and guide the clients vision. Through the development of high fidelity wireframing and active collaboration with our development team, we were able to take their needs and build an end-to-end production ready product that is being used right now.
